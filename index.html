Welcome

Welcome to Education Simple E-Learning, a visionary initiative of C.A.P. Consultants, LLC, under the guidance of founder Consuelo Palacio, PhD. Our mission is clear: to share invaluable subject matter expertise cultivated from real-life experiences with individuals eager to expand their knowledge base.

Consuelo's journey began amidst the hustle and bustle of a dynamic urban setting, where she delved into various aspects of subject matter learning. With a passion for technology and web development, she seamlessly integrated online instruction and training into her repertoire, refining her methods through seminar training and coursework at Tarrant County Community College. Consuelo proudly holds a doctorate in urban education administration from the esteemed University of Wisconsin - Milwaukee.

Now, you have the opportunity to tap into Consuelo's wealth of knowledge and expertise from the comfort of your own home, with no compromise on the quality of subject content.
